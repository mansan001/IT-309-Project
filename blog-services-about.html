Mikaela Dela Cruz

the head of Mikrafts

Meet Mikaela, the creative mind behind Mikrafts, a thriving company dedicated to crafting exquisite crocheted items. With a passion for both artistry and entrepreneurship, Mikaela embarked on her journey to share her love for crochet with the world. Armed with needles, yarn, and boundless imagination, she set out to create a brand that celebrates craftsmanship, creativity, and individuality.

Driven by her passion for crochet and fueled by the support of her loyal customers, Mikaela continues to expand Mikrafts, spreading joy and warmth one stitch at a time. Through her company, she hopes to inspire others to embrace their creativity, pursue their passions, and find beauty in the art of handmade craftsmanship.
